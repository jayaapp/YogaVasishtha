# पट
**Transliteration**: pa-ṭa
**POS**: Masculine noun

**Root Analysis**:
- From √पट् (paṭ) meaning "to split, tear, divide"
- Suffix -अ forming noun

**Meanings**:
- Literal: cloth, fabric, woven material
- Contextual: material veil of illusion in Advaitic philosophy
- Symbolic: the apparent reality that conceals the absolute truth

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember "pattern" - cloth has patterns like maya has patterns of illusion
