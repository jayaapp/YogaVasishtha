# धीमहि
**Transliteration**: dhī-ma-hi
**POS**: verb (1st person plural, middle voice, present tense)

**Root Analysis**:
- √धी (dhī) – "to think, contemplate, meditate" + महि (mahi) – 1st person plural middle ending = "we contemplate/meditate"

**Meanings**:
- Literal: we think, we contemplate, we meditate upon
- Contextual: used in invocatory contexts in the Yoga Vasishtha for collective meditation
- Symbolic: represents unified consciousness in group spiritual practice

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember as "dhee-mahi" - collective meditation practice

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Represents collective consciousness (saṃghāta caitanya)
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Group meditation enhancing individual sādhana
- **Dharmic Significance**: Unity of individual and collective awareness
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Gāyatrī Mantra and Yoga Vasishtha meditation sections

- **Dharmic Significance**: Established in self-knowledge (ātmajñāna)
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Bhagavad Gītā (sthitaprajña) and Yoga Vasishtha
