# तेजः
**Transliteration**: te-jaḥ
**POS**: noun [neuter], nominative singular

**Root Analysis**:
- √तिज् (tij) – "to be sharp, shine" + अस् (as) – "nominal suffix" = "sharpness, brilliance, fire"

**Meanings**:
- Literal: fire, brilliance, radiance, sharpness, energy
- Contextual: One of the five elements (pañcamahābhūta); divine radiance and spiritual energy
- Symbolic: The luminous principle of consciousness; transformative spiritual fire

**Learning Tip**:
- तेजस् is the fire element, third in the sequence: ākāśa, vāyu, tejas, āpas, pṛthvī

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Third cosmic element representing fire, light, and transformation
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Associated with maṇipūra cakra and digestive fire (agni)
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Agni as divine fire; Sūrya as cosmic radiance
- **Mantric Significance**: Fire mantras and solar practices; RAM bīja mantra
- **Dharmic Significance**: Spiritual energy that burns away impurities and ignorance
- **Symbolic Representation**: Sacred fire, solar disc, lightning, inner luminosity
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Taittirīya Upanishad on cosmic elements; Agni Purāṇa
