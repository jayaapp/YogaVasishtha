# सर्ब्बभूतेषु
**Transliteration**: sar-va-bhū-te-ṣu
**POS**: Locative plural of compound noun

**Root Analysis**:
- सर्व (sarva) "all" + भूत (bhūta) "beings/elements" + locative plural suffix -eṣu
- From √भू (bhū) "to be, become" + participial suffix -ta

**Meanings**:
- Literal: In all beings/elements
- Contextual: Universal presence in all manifestations
- Symbolic: Omnipresence of consciousness

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember सर्व = "all" and भूत = "beings" for universal concepts

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Expression of universal consciousness
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Recognition of divine presence everywhere
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Vishnu's omnipresence
- **Mantric Significance**: Invocation of universal awareness
- **Dharmic Significance**: Non-dual recognition
- **Symbolic Representation**: All-pervading light
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Bhagavad Gita, Yoga Vasishtha
