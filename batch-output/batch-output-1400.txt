# महीयते
**Transliteration**: ma-hī-ya-te
**POS**: Verb (3rd person singular, passive/middle voice, present tense)

**Root Analysis**:
- Root: √मह् (mah) - to honor, magnify, worship
- Suffix: -ईय (īya) - passive/potential participle marker
- Ending: -ते (te) - 3rd person singular middle/passive

**Meanings**:
- Literal: is honored, is magnified, is revered
- Contextual: refers to the reverence given to divine consciousness in Yoga Vasishtha
- Symbolic: the natural tendency of consciousness to honor its own supreme nature

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember "mah" as "magnificent" - what becomes magnificent is honored

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: The process by which universal consciousness recognizes its own glory
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Devotional practices that magnify divine awareness
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Associated with Mahatma (great soul) principle
- **Mantric Significance**: Invokes reverence in sacred recitation
- **Dharmic Significance**: The soul's natural inclination to honor the Self
- **Symbolic Representation**: Depicted as flames rising upward in reverence
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Yoga Vasishtha, Bhagavata Purana
