# ब्रह्माण्डञ्चापिकोटिशः
**Transliteration**: brah-mā-ṇḍañ-cā-pi-ko-ṭi-śaḥ
**POS**: Compound noun with enclitic particles

**Root Analysis**:
- ब्रह्मण्ड (brahmaṇḍa): ब्रह्म (brahman) + अण्ड (aṇḍa, egg/universe)
- च (ca): enclitic "and"
- अपि (api): particle "even/also"
- कोटि (koṭi): "ten million, countless"
- शः suffix indicating multiplicity

**Meanings**:
- Literal: And even countless millions of universes
- Contextual: Reference to infinite cosmic manifestation in Yoga Vasishtha
- Symbolic: The limitless nature of brahman's creative power

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember ब्रह्माण्ड as "cosmic egg" - the universe as brahman's manifestation

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Infinite multiplicity within unified consciousness
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Meditation on cosmic vastness
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Virat-purusha, cosmic form
- **Mantric Significance**: Contemplation of Om as cosmic sound
- **Dharmic Significance**: Recognition of brahman's infinite manifestation
- **Symbolic Representation**: Multiple nested spheres in cosmic diagrams
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Puranas, Yoga Vasishtha
