# प्रणबेन
**Transliteration**: pra-ṇa-ve-na
**POS**: Instrumental case form

**Root Analysis**:
- From प्रणव (praṇava) + इन (instrumental suffix)
- प्र (pra) = forth, forward + णव (ṇava) from √नु (to praise)

**Meanings**:
- Literal: By means of praṇava, through Om
- Contextual: Instrumental use of the sacred syllable in meditation
- Symbolic: The means by which cosmic consciousness is accessed

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember instrumental case shows "by means of" - the Om syllable as spiritual tool

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: The instrument for cosmic realization
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Primary mantra for meditation practice
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Brahman as sound-form
- **Mantric Significance**: Core seed sound for all mantras
- **Dharmic Significance**: Gateway to transcendent consciousness
- **Symbolic Representation**: Symbol ॐ as cosmic gateway
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Mandukya Upanishad, Yoga Sutras
