# अधिभूम्य
**Transliteration**: a-dhi-bhū-mya
**POS**: adjective

**Root Analysis**:
- adhi- "over, above" + √bhū (bhū) "to be" + -ya suffix = "relating to what is above the earth"

**Meanings**:
- Literal: relating to the earthly realm, terrestrial
- Contextual: one of the threefold aspects of existence (adhibhūta)
- Symbolic: material plane of manifestation

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember as the material/earthly aspect in the threefold cosmic classification

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Material aspect of existence in the threefold division
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Understanding gross material plane
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Earth element deities and energies
- **Mantric Significance**: Mantras for grounding and material harmony
- **Dharmic Significance**: Understanding the physical as divine manifestation
- **Symbolic Representation**: Earth symbols, squares, physical forms
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Vedantic classification texts
