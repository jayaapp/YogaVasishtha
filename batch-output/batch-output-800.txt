# धारा
**Transliteration**: dhā-rā
**POS**: noun [feminine]

**Root Analysis**:
- √धृ (dhṛ) – "to hold, flow" + आ (ā) – "feminine suffix" = "flow, stream, edge"

**Meanings**:
- Literal: flow, stream, current, edge of a blade
- Contextual: Continuous flow of consciousness or divine grace
- Symbolic: The unbroken stream of spiritual awareness

**Learning Tip**:
- धारा refers to continuous flow - like stream of water or consciousness

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Continuous flow of cosmic consciousness and divine grace
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Unbroken flow of awareness in advanced meditation
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Divine grace flowing continuously to devotees
- **Mantric Significance**: Continuous stream of sacred sound in advanced practice
- **Dharmic Significance**: Unbroken flow of ethical awareness and action
- **Symbolic Representation**: The endless river of divine consciousness
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Descriptions of continuous awareness in meditation texts
