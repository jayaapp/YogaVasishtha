# रक्षणे
**Transliteration**: rak-ṣa-ṇe
**POS**: Noun (locative singular)

**Root Analysis**:
- Root: √रक्ष् (rakṣ) - to protect, guard, preserve
- Form: रक्षण (rakṣaṇa) - protection, preservation + locative ending -e

**Meanings**:
- Literal: in protection, in preservation
- Contextual: regarding the safeguarding of consciousness or spiritual practices
- Symbolic: the protective aspect of divine grace

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember "rakṣā" (protection) - the locative shows "in the act of protecting"

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Divine protection of the spiritual aspirant
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Protective mantras and spiritual shields
- **Deity or Energy Form**: Protective deities like Hanuman
- **Mantric Significance**: Kavach mantras for spiritual protection
- **Dharmic Significance**: Protection of dharma and righteous path
- **Symbolic Representation**: Protective yantras and amulets
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Yoga Vasishtha, Ramayana
