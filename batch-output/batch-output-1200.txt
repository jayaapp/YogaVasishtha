# बिजरो
**Transliteration**: bi-ja-ro
**POS**: Adjective/Noun

**Root Analysis**:
- Root: बिज (bija) + रो (ro) - "seed" + "destroyer/remover"
- Morphology: Compound formation indicating one who destroys or removes seeds

**Meanings**:
- Literal: Seedless, barren, sterile
- Contextual: State of consciousness without karmic seeds
- Symbolic: Liberation from cycles of manifestation

**Learning Tip**:
- Remember as "beyond seeds" - transcending causal patterns

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: State of consciousness beyond karmic causation
- **Energetic/Yogic Application**: Advanced state in meditation where karmic seeds are exhausted
- **Dharmic Significance**: Liberation from cycles of cause and effect
- **Symbolic Representation**: Empty vessel or void space
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Yoga Vasishtha, Advaita texts
