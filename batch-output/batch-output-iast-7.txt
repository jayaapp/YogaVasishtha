# चित्
**Transliteration**: chit
**POS**: noun (feminine, stem form)

**Root Analysis**:
- √cit (cit) "to perceive, be conscious" = "consciousness"

**Meanings**:
- Literal: consciousness, awareness
- Contextual: the fundamental conscious principle
- Symbolic: the eternal witness of all experience

**Learning Tip**:
- One of the three fundamental aspects of Brahman (sat-cit-ānanda)

**Metaphysics**:
- **Cosmic Role**: Pure consciousness aspect of ultimate reality
- **Dharmic Significance**: The unchanging awareness in which all experience appears
- **Primary Textual Attestation**: Throughout Vedantic literature
